# Scout — One-Week Marketing Campaign Plan

**Campaign Name:** *Your Network Is Bigger Than You Think*
**Duration:** Monday, July 13 – Sunday, July 19 (mid-summer, pre-fall sports ramp-up)
**Overall Goal:** +25% student-athlete signups, +15% alumni profile claims, 10% increase in weekly active users

---

## Target Audiences

### 1. Incoming Freshmen (Class of 2030)
- **Who they are:** Cornell '30 admits arriving on East Hill this fall. Anxious about finding their footing. Don't yet understand that being an athlete unlocks a 55-year head start.
- **Pain point:** "I have no connections in finance/tech/consulting. How do I even start?"
- **Emotional hook:** You're not starting from zero. You're inheriting 18,000+ Big Red alumni who wore the same jersey.
- **Key message:** "Walk onto campus already connected. Every Cornell athlete before you is your network — you just haven't met them yet."

### 2. Current Student-Athletes (Sophomores, Juniors, Seniors)
- **Who they are:** Roster athletes actively recruiting for internships and full-time roles. Some are already using Scout; many haven't heard of it or haven't been onboarded.
- **Pain point:** "My teammates who graduated are at Goldman/Meta/McKinsey. I know they'd help, but cold-messaging feels awkward."
- **Emotional hook:** That upperclassman who showed you the ropes as a freshman? They're now at a top firm. Scout turns that relationship into a warm intro.
- **Key message:** "Stop Googling 'Cornell alum at [Company].' Open Scout. Your network is already in your pocket."

### 3. Alumni
- **Who they are:** Former Cornell athletes now at Goldman, McKinsey, Google, Bridgewater, etc. 35–55 years old. Proud of their Big Red identity. Want to give back but don't have time for structured mentorship.
- **Pain point:** "I'd help a Cornell athlete in a heartbeat, but I don't have bandwidth for another formal mentorship program."
- **Emotional hook:** One 5-minute LinkedIn message can change a kid's life. No meetings, no long-term commitment — just be in the directory and reply when a student reaches out.
- **Key message:** "Remember who helped you get your first job. Be that person now. Join Scout — it takes 2 minutes."

## Highlighting New Features

- **Where to emphasize:** Email to coaches/athletic department, LinkedIn

## Metrics to Track

### Acquisition Metrics
- Daily new user signups (student-athlete vs. alumni)
- Signup source attribution (Instagram, LinkedIn, email, team chat, direct)
- Incoming freshmen signup rate
- Alumni profile claims (new + existing)

### Engagement Metrics
- Daily active users (DAU)
- Weekly active users (WAU)
- Profiles viewed per session
- Alumni saved to network
- Messages sent (AI-generated + manual)
- Swipe-to-save conversion rate
- Circles feature usage (network visualization)

### Retention Metrics
- Day-7 retention rate
- Day-30 retention rate
- Campaign completion rate (students who message X alumni)
- Alumni reply rate to student messages

### Feature-Specific Metrics
- Flag submissions (volume, resolution time)
- Admin dashboard logins (coach engagement)
- Page load times (Redis performance improvement)
- Brand sentiment (comment analysis, NPS survey pinned to app)

### Conversion Metrics
- Signup → onboarding completion rate
- Onboarding → first discovery session rate
- Saved alumni → message sent rate
- Message sent → alumni reply rate
- Reply → interview scheduled rate
