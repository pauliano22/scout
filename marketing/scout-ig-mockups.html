Education · Format B

How to get an alum to reply

Most outreach gets ignored for the same two reasons, and both are fixable. The first is being vague, because "I'd love to learn about your career" gives a stranger nothing to say yes to. The second is asking for too much, since nobody mentors a person they've never met for an hour. Name the one thing you want to know, keep it to fifteen minutes, and watch how many people say yes. Save this and steal it for your next message. #Cornell #CornellAthletics #BigRed #StudentAthlete #Networking
